Research Statement

My research investigates how retrieval systems can move beyond semantic similarity toward utility-aware, context-sensitive, and interpretable reasoning.

I aim to develop neurosymbolic approaches that improve the reliability of AI systems, particularly in high-stakes domains where incorrect information can have serious consequences.
