Hi, I’m Salome Chemiat

I research how to make AI systems more reliable, safe, and context-aware, especially in high-stakes domains like healthcare.

How can AI move from retrieving similar information to retrieving useful and safe knowledge?

About

My work focuses on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), Natural Language Processing, and Neurosymbolic AI. I study how current retrieval systems fail when semantic similarity does not match real-world utility.

I am particularly interested in healthcare AI systems, where incorrect outputs can have serious consequences. My research explores robustness, failure-mode analysis, and symbolic reasoning as a corrective layer for neural systems.
