About

I am an early-stage researcher interested in developing AI systems that move beyond pattern matching toward more reliable, interpretable, and context-aware reasoning.

My work focuses on retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), particularly on addressing a key limitation: semantic similarity does not always correspond to useful or appropriate responses, especially in high-stakes domains such as healthcare.

Research Statement

My research aims to improve retrieval mechanisms by moving beyond semantic similarity toward utility-aware, interpretable, and context-sensitive approaches. I am particularly interested in integrating neural retrieval with symbolic reasoning to improve reliability in high-stakes domains such as healthcare.
